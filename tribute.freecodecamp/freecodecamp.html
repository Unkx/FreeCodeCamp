Dr. Norman Osborn

Amazing Scientist ,and a great father.



Timeline of Dr. Osborn's life:

  • 1930 - Born in Manhattan, New York

  • 1947 - Norman leaves family to study at Harvard University

  • 1953 - After getting his master degree, Norman decided to act charity by going to Kenya

  • 1962 - Osborn decided to come back to USA to obtain a doctoral degree in the field of nanotechnology

  • 1965 - Norman was hired in a nano-technology company

  • 1980 - With needed experience,Norman decided to build his own company-Oscorp.

  • 1996 - Osborn was killed by a bandit who thought he was a spiderman
